Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our clothing, sizing, shipping, and more.

Size & Fit

How do I find my correct size?

We provide detailed size charts for all our products. You can find the size guide link on each product page. Our sizing runs true to fit, but we recommend checking the measurements in the size chart as different styles may fit differently. If you're between sizes, consider sizing up for a more comfortable fit.

Do your Islamic clothing items fit modestly?

Yes! All our clothing is designed with modesty in mind. Our items feature appropriate lengths, coverage, and fits. We offer abayas, hijabs, modest dresses, and loose-fitting styles specifically designed to meet Islamic dress standards while maintaining a contemporary, stylish appearance.

What's your return policy for sizing issues?

We offer a 30-day return policy on all items. If the size doesn't fit as expected, you can return it for a full refund or exchange it for the correct size. Items must be in original condition with tags attached. Return shipping is free for sizing exchanges.

Fabric & Care

What materials do you use in your clothing?

We use premium materials including cotton, silk blends, polyester, and linen. Each product description details the exact fabric composition. We prioritize comfort and durability, sourcing high-quality materials that are breathable, long-lasting, and suitable for everyday wear.

How should I care for my Islamic clothing?

Care instructions are included on each product's care label. Generally, we recommend:

  • Wash in cold water with similar colors
  • Use gentle detergent for delicate fabrics
  • Avoid bleach and harsh chemicals
  • Air dry when possible; tumble dry on low heat if needed
  • Iron on low to medium heat as directed

Will my clothing shrink?

Most of our items are pre-shrunk and designed to maintain their size with proper care. Some natural fabrics like 100% cotton may shrink slightly (1-2%) if washed in hot water and high heat drying. To minimize shrinkage, wash in cold water and air dry when possible.

Shipping & Delivery

How long does shipping take?

We offer multiple shipping options:

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days
  • Overnight Shipping: Next business day
Processing time is 1-2 business days. Shipping times are estimates and may vary during peak seasons.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 100 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location. Customs and import duties may apply for some countries and are the responsibility of the customer. You can see shipping costs at checkout before placing your order.

Can I track my order?

Yes! Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your package in real-time on the carrier's website. You'll also receive shipping updates on your account page.

Payments & Orders

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept:

  • Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay
  • Google Pay

All payments are processed securely using SSL encryption.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your payment information. We never store full credit card numbers on our servers. Your payment is processed securely through trusted payment gateways.

Can I cancel or modify my order?

If your order hasn't shipped yet, you can cancel or modify it. Please contact our customer service team immediately with your order number. Once your order has shipped, it cannot be cancelled, but you can return it for a refund or exchange.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on all items. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with tags attached. Return shipping is free. Once we receive your return, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days.

How do I initiate a return?

To start a return:

  • Log into your account and go to "My Orders"
  • Select the item you wish to return
  • Click "Return Item" and follow the instructions
  • Print your free return shipping label
  • Pack your item securely and ship it back

Can I exchange items?

Yes! You can exchange items for a different size or color. Free return shipping is provided for exchanges. If the new item costs more, you'll pay the difference; if less, we'll refund the difference.

Halal & Certification

Are your products Halal certified?

Our clothing items comply with Islamic dress standards. While our apparel itself is not "Halal certified" (as that term applies to food and consumables), we ensure our designs meet Islamic modesty requirements. Our perfumes are crafted to be alcohol-free and suitable for Islamic practice.

Are your fabrics ethically sourced?

We are committed to ethical sourcing and fair labor practices. We work with suppliers who meet high environmental and labor standards. We're continuously improving our supply chain transparency and sustainability practices.
